Computer Science Program
January 26th, 2016

I created my own completely free computer science program. I am still working through it and plan to complete it by the middle of 2017.

I graduated from George Mason University in August of 2014 with a degree in Economics but wanted to be a Software Developer. I landed a job as a Junior Dev 6 months after graduating, but for the entire year of 2015 I felt like I lacked all the fundamentals of computer science. I wanted to go back to school but after getting frustrated with high tuition costs (I already had enough debt from my first bachelors), I turned to free online courses via coursera, MIT, iTunes U, etc. I hope someone else finds this program fun and useful!
